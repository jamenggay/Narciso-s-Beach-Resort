Events

  • Venue Rental

    Looking for the perfect setting for your special occasion? Our scenic beachside venue offers an unforgettable backdrop for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and more.

  • Friday Dance Night

    Kick off your weekend with the ultimate beachside dance party at our Friday Dance Night! Feel the energy as live DJs spin your favorite tracks, filling the air with exciting beats and vibrant lights that bring the night to life.

  • Acoustic Night

    Unwind with an evening of soulful tunes and serene ocean views at our Acoustic Night. Every Wednesday, our beachfront transforms into a cozy, intimate space where talented local musicians perform acoustic sets under the stars.

  • Open Mic

    Step into the spotlight and showcase your talent at our Open Mic night! Whether you're a singer, poet, comedian, or musician, our welcoming beachside stage is the perfect place to share your art.

  • Live Music

    Get ready for an electrifying night at our Live Music events! Every weekend, we bring the best local bands and artists to our beachfront stage, creating an unforgettable experience filled with incredible performances and vibrant energy.
