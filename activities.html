Activities

Banana Boat Ride

Get ready for an exhilarating beach adventure with our banana boat ride—the ultimate thrill for family and friends! For only ₱200 - ₱500 per person , enjoy 15 to 20 minutes of fast-paced fun as our banana boat zips across the sparkling ocean waves, and plenty of laughter.

Jet Ski

Rev up your beach adventure with an exciting jet ski ride! For just ₱1,500 - ₱3,000 per 30 minutes , experience the thrill of riding the waves on a high-powered jet ski, perfect for solo riders or those wanting to share the excitement with a friend.

Kayak Ride

Discover the beauty of the ocean at your own pace with our kayak rentals! For just ₱200 - ₱500 per hour , glide across the calm waters, explore hidden coves, and enjoy stunning views as you paddle through paradise.

Beach Volleyball

Gather your friends and spike up the excitement with a game of beach volleyball! For only ₱100 - ₱300 per hour, enjoy a sandy court and scenic beach views as you dive, set, and smash your way to victory.

Amenities

Infinity Pool

Dive into endless relaxation with our stunning infinity pool, designed to blend seamlessly with the surrounding ocean and sky. Located just steps away from the beach, this luxurious pool offers breathtaking, panoramic views that make it feel as if you’re swimming right into the horizon. Lounge in the sun on our comfortable poolside chairs, or take a refreshing dip while enjoying the serene sound of the waves. Whether you’re here for a sunrise swim or a sunset view, our infinity pool provides the perfect backdrop for unwinding in style.
