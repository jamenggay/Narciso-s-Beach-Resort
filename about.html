About Us

Partially opened in November 2022, Narciso's Beach Resort is a family-owned business located in the heart of the robust and rural community of Alisyao, Mondragon, N. Samar. It is managed by the NCAL Corporation, a construction and quarrying company owned by the Lim family.

Narciso's Beach Resort's primary mission and vision is to provide guests with exemplary service and proper accommodations within the humble province of Northern Samar.

We aim to help our community's tourism flourish by providing guests with a comfortable place t stay while exploring the beautiful and unseen natural wonders of Northern Samar.
